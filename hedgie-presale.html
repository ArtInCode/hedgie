The Game

Giddy up! Journey with your Hedgie through four unique worlds: Desert, Forest, Wetlands, and Mountains. Along the way, you’ll find all sorts of goodies that boost performance and help you earn coin.

But first, you have to choose your one-of-a-kind Hedgie.

There are four types of Hedgies: Earth, Air, Water, and Fire. Every Hedgie has special powers that determine your Hedgie’s performance in each of the four worlds.

Hey, what’s this!?

Along its journey, Hedgie will find different artifacts that it can consume, collect, or exchange for Curio (cryptocurrency). This includes edibles, non-edibles, and tools that help Hedgie perform daily tasks and increase your chances of success.
