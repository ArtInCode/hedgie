How to Install MetaMask:

1

Google Chrome, visit https://metamask.io and click Get Chrome Extension.

2

Click Add to Chrome.

3

Click Add Extension.

4

Click on the MetaMask icon in the top right of the window.

5

Click Accept. Read the TERMS OF USE and click Accept again.

6

Enter and confirm your password, then click Create.

7

Copy the twelve words that appear and save them somewhere safe.

8

This is your MetaMask wallet.

9

Click on the three dots next to Account 1, and click View Account on Etherscan.

10

A new tab will open. View your wallet address here.
